Charles Babbage

Charles Babbage was born in London December 26th 1791 and died in London October 18th 1871 Charles' invention changed the world of technology forever as he is credited to have created the first ever automatic digital computer in 1832.

Charles Babbage Top 3 Facts

  • Founded the Analytical Society to help introduce developments from Europe into traditional English math.
  • Elected to the Royal Society of London in 1816
  • Helped in the founding of the Royal Astronomical and the Statistical societies.
